PhD Program

About the Program

The Department of Business Administration at Hacettepe University has been offering a PhD program since 1974. The aim of the doctoral program is to ensure that students acquire the necessary theoretical and scientific knowledge in their area of specialization, conduct independent and ethical research, examine scientific findings and events from a broad and in-depth perspective, reach new syntheses, develop universally valid new methods, and produce new knowledge.

Our PhD program, in addition to offering theoretical and applied courses covering all disciplines of business administration, provides students with opportunities to engage in scientific research and be involved in academic studies—and requires them to do so. Our faculty members and entire academic staff are recognized nationally and internationally, follow the most current developments in academic curricula, apply these to their teaching programs, and closely monitor student development.

In the University Research Laboratory's "State Universities and Faculties Ranking," which ranks state universities and their faculties in Turkey based on academic incentive performance, our Department of Business Administration ranked first in the "Faculty of Economics and Administrative Sciences / Business Administration / Economics / Management Sciences Ranking" in 2020 with the highest score. This ranking evaluated the academic performance of faculty members covering project, research, publication, design, exhibition, patent, citation, paper, and award activity types, certifying the quality of our department's academic staff.

Our doctoral program is open to all students who wish to specialize in different areas of business, conduct scientific studies in these fields, and who are curious, enthusiastic, and possess strong analytical and interpretive skills.

Program Structure

The Hacettepe University Business Administration PhD Program consists of three main stages:

  • Coursework Stage: During the coursework stage, students must take the following compulsory courses covering all disciplines of business within the first two academic years: "Research Methods I, Organization and Managerial Behavior, Finance Theories, Strategic Planning and Management, Marketing Strategy Management, Mathematical Programming, and Seminar." All other courses are elective in nature. Students must successfully complete a total of 120 ECTS credits (90 ECTS + 30 ECTS Qualifying Exam) by taking elective courses from their area of specialization.
  • Qualifying Exam Stage: Students who successfully complete 90 ECTS credits from the compulsory and elective course pool earn the right to take the qualifying exam. This exam includes written and oral examination stages and covers all areas of business, with emphasis on the student's main field of study. Students have two attempts for this exam.
  • Thesis Writing Stage: Students who pass the qualifying exam proceed to the doctoral thesis preparation stage, where a thesis advisor and thesis monitoring committee are assigned. Candidates must complete their thesis within 4 semesters and pass the thesis defense examination. Before the thesis defense, students must have at least one research article—related to their thesis and as first author—published or accepted in journals indexed in SSCI, SCI, SCI-Expanded, AHCI, or in journals listed in the ULAKBİM TR Index during their doctoral study period.

Research Areas

The Hacettepe University PhD Program offers six areas of specialization and research from the core disciplines of business:

  • Accounting and Finance
  • Marketing
  • Organizational Behavior
  • Quantitative Methods
  • Production and Operations Management
  • Management and Organization

Resources

In our department, a computer laboratory and an optimization and simulation laboratory are available for doctoral program students to use as workspaces upon request, subject to availability. Students can access all licensed software made available through the Hacettepe University Software Repository System. Doctoral students can access the electronic resources to which Hacettepe University Libraries subscribe, both on-campus and off-campus (IP-controlled).

Students wishing to learn more about Hacettepe University's physical and social facilities and campus amenities can visit the H.U. Sustainable Campuses Coordinatorship Official Web Page.
