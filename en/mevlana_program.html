Mevlana Exchange Program

The Mevlana Exchange Program is a program that aims to facilitate student and academic staff exchange between domestic and international higher education institutions. With the regulation published in the Official Gazette No. 28034 dated August 23, 2011, the path was opened for exchange between higher education institutions abroad and those in our country.

Students wishing to participate in the program can benefit from the program for a minimum of one and a maximum of two semesters for education; academic staff can benefit for a minimum of one week and a maximum of three months to teach at higher education institutions worldwide. Similarly, students and academic staff from all regions of the world can come to higher education institutions in Turkey to participate in educational activities.

About the Mevlana Program

The Mevlana Program is an exchange system that supports the internationalization process of higher education institutions in Turkey and promotes academic cooperation worldwide. Through the program, students gain the opportunity to experience different cultures and acquire a global perspective.

Hacettepe University collaborates with universities from many countries within the framework of the Mevlana Program. For detailed information, please visit the official Mevlana web page.
