Farabi Exchange Program

The Farabi Exchange Program is a national exchange program aimed at facilitating student and faculty member exchange between higher education institutions. The program supports student and faculty mobility among associate, bachelor's, master's, and doctoral level programs at universities and high technology institutes in Turkey.

Within the scope of the program, students can continue their education at a higher education institution other than their own for one or two semesters. Similarly, faculty members can also find opportunities to teach and engage in academic activities at different universities.

What is the Farabi Program?

Also known as the Student and Faculty Member Exchange Program Between Higher Education Institutions, Farabi aims to broaden students' perspectives by providing education in different academic environments and allowing them to experience different institutional cultures.

Students who meet the application requirements can apply during the periods announced by the Hacettepe University Farabi Program Coordinatorship.
