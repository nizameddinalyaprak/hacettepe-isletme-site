Erasmus+ Program

The Erasmus+ program is a European Union program aimed at encouraging cooperation among higher education institutions. Within this program, higher education institutions can carry out joint projects, receive non-repayable financial support for short-term student and staff exchanges, and improve the employability of university graduates in the business world.
