Internship

As is known, an internship during university education is a practical learning period in which students undergo training to acquire professional skills. During this period, students work in one or more divisions of an organization to enhance both their theoretical and professional knowledge.

The internship period is an important process where students observe how their academic knowledge acquired during their education is applied in organizational management systems, actively participate in work activities to develop their knowledge and skills, and take their first steps toward a career. Internships done during university provide students with the opportunity to practice while their knowledge is still fresh.

Important Documents and Rules

Internship Course Principles and Rules
Changes to Rules

Students who are entitled to do an internship within the scope of the National Internship Program must contact the department Internship Coordinator to submit the required documents accurately, completely, and promptly, so that insurance procedures can be carried out by our university.

Students who declared they would take an internship but later decided not to must promptly contact the internship coordinator and submit their petition to the department secretary. Notification must be made at least 3 business days before the internship start date.

Application Process

1 month before the internship start date, documents should be filled in digitally, with wet signature and stamp, scanned and submitted via e-mail.

Insurance Covered by University

  • Copy of ID Card (Annex 1)
  • Intern Student Information Form (Annex 2)
  • Workplace Application and Acceptance Form (Annex 3)
  • Declaration and Undertaking (Annex 4 or Annex 5)

Insurance Covered by the Institution

  • Copy of ID Card (Annex 1)
  • SSI Employment Registration (Annex 6)
  • Institution Letter (Annex 7)

** The employment registration must include the term "intern".

For International Students

International students are required to obtain work permit exemption documents. Procedures are carried out through emuafiyet.csgb.gov.tr.

Internship Report and Submission

The internship report must comply with the preparation guide and each page must be stamped/signed by the institution. The submission deadline is the final exam week.
