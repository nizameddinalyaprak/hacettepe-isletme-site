Our Mission

To provide a modern, research-based, and ethically grounded education in management, marketing, accounting-finance, and quantitative methods; to cultivate critical-thinking, interdisciplinary managers and researchers who add value to society; and to advance knowledge production through national and international projects.

Our Vision

To become a leading Department of Business Administration in Turkey and the world, distinguished by scientific output, quality of education, and societal impact; fostering a boundary-pushing, ethical, and inclusive academic culture.
